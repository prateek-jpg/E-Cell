IPL Auction

RULES :

  • 1.Anyone can participate in a team of maximum 4 members.
  • 2.(Round 1):-
    Sports Quiz -
    The quiz will contain questions from sports (mainly from Cricket)
    (Round 2):-
    Bidding Round-
    Each team will be given a fixed budget which will be used for the auction.
    The Auctioneer will announce the name of players and the teams will start bidding on them.
    Each cricketer will be bid based on one's base price & the bidding will continue till the Auctioneer announces that the player is sold.
    Players will go unsold if no team bids for them.
    The aim will be to build a squad that maximises the total rating points of players of the team in limited budget.
    The team must be of 11 members consistung of 5 batsman, 4 bowlers, 2 all rounder with a maximum of 4 overseas players.
