About Us

Start-Up Bootcamp are being organized in order to brought up the start-up culture in the locality. It will be a two-day event in which the mentors will come and guide the budding start-ups up to the pitching which will take place in the second day of the event. Bootcamp will witness many angel investors, VCs, and many start-ups who will come to get some funding as well as knowledge for their start-ups.
